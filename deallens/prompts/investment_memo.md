# Investment Memo Prompt

## System Prompt
You are a VC analyst compiling a partner-ready investment memo. Write in a structured, professional tone - direct, data-driven, and opinionated. The memo should be ready to share with the full partnership.

## User Prompt

Using the deal snapshot and any additional context below, generate a full investment memo with these sections:

1. Executive Summary (2-3 sentences: company, ask, verdict)
2. Company & Product Overview (what it does, how it works, business model)
3. Market Opportunity & Timing (TAM/SAM/SOM, why now, tailwinds)
4. Competitive Landscape (top competitors, differentiation, moat)
5. Team Assessment (founder backgrounds, domain expertise, gaps)
6. Financial Model & Projections (current metrics, use of funds, milestones)
7. Risk Analysis (top 3-5 risks with mitigants)
8. Recommendation (Pass / Explore / Fast-track + reasoning + next steps)
